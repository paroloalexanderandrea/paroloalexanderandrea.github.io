OUR WINE

Our sparkling wine is a metodo classico, also known as champenoise, brut, cuvee of 100% Chardonnay. The extra-fine perlage and persistance make it a wine for important occasions, great for appetizers, idealy paired with a fish based menu.

The wine is obtained only from our finest vineyards, selection which of is done in respect to precise rules, defined by the attentive work of the vineyard technitians.

From the refermentation begin 24 long months of fining on the yeasts necessary for the wine to mature and for the peculiarities of the product to emerge.

CEMBRA VALLEY

Heroic viticolture practiced for millennia, the fruit of mans work, which in the utter most respect of the environment, has managed to steal cultivable terrain from the mountains, thanks to the creation of terracings, substained by stone walls, for a total of 700 km.

The protagonist is also the soil, with a geological matrix mainly made up of effusive volcanic rock that in hundreds of thousands of years has gone to make up a very particular terrain, consisting mainly of boulders and pebbles, with a good presence of limestone and sand, accomplices of mineral elegance and the subtle, sharp harmony of the nectars of this area.

OUR STORY

We are a group of 5 students from the Agricultural Technical Institute of San Michele all'Adige,future wine technicians who decided to put ourselves to the test by applying all the knowledge acquired so far to make a classic method sparkling wine. From the basics to the disgorgement, without neglecting the search for bottles, the design of the labels and the search for all the materials necessary for the presentation of the final product.
